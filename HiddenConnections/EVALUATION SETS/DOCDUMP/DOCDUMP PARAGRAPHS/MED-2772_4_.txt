Three major dietary patterns were found: 1) "animal products and fried food," 2) "western," 3) "soybean products and fruit." In multivariate analyses the "animal products and fried food" pattern score was associated with an odds ratio (OR) of 2.15 (95% CI, 1.22-3.76) compared with the lowest tertile. The OR for the top tertile of score for "soybean products and fruit" pattern was 0.32 (95% CI, 0.19-0.57) compared with the lowest tertile of "soybean products and fruit" pattern score.
