Serum samples were analyzed for estrone (E1) and estradiol (E2) using radioimmunoassays. Nine estrogen metabolites were measured in urine by liquid chromatography mass spectrometry. Semi-vegetarians included women who reported <30 g/day of red meat, poultry, and fish and pescatarians who consumed <20 g/day of meat/poultry but >10 g/day of fish.
