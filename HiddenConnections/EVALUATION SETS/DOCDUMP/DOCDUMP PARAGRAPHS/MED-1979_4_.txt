Using sophisticated mass spectrometry, these compounds as well as free galactose can be measured in plasma and in urine. It is clear that initiation of dietary restriction of galactose in the newborn period produces reversal of hepatic, renal, brain, and immune dysfunction, along with reduction of the accumulated galactose metabolites. However, the neurologist should be aware that chronic and progressive neurologic impairments occur even in patients spared these neonatal symptoms.
