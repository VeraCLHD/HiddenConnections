All men had a complete examination of the cardiovascular system and a resting electrocardiogram (ECG). Systolic BP (SBP) > or = 140 mmHg was found in 42.6% of the subjects in 1960 and in 45.2% in 1991 (NS). Diastolic BP > or = 95 mmHG was found in 14.9% of the subjects in 1960 as opposed to 33.3% in 1991 (P < 0.02).
