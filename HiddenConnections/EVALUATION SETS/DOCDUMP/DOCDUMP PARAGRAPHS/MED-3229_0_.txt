Abstract OBJECTIVE: To evaluate the impact of midlife elevated serum cholesterol levels and blood pressure on the subsequent development of mild cognitive impairment (MCI) and to investigate the prevalence of MCI in elderly Finnish population, applying the MCI criteria devised by the Mayo Clinic Alzheimer's Disease Research Center. BACKGROUND: MCI has been considered as a predictor of AD. Vascular risk factors may be important in the development of cognitive impairment and AD.
