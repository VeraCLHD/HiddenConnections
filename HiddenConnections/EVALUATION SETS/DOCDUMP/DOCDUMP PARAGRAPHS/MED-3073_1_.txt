PURPOSE: To review evidence related to knowledge gaps identified by the 2002 recommendation and to consider community performance of screening endoscopy, including harms. DATA SOURCES: MEDLINE, Cochrane Library, expert suggestions, and bibliographic reviews. STUDY SELECTION: Eligible studies reported performance of colorectal cancer screening tests or health outcomes in average-risk populations and were at least of fair quality according to design-specific USPSTF criteria, as determined by 2 reviewers.
