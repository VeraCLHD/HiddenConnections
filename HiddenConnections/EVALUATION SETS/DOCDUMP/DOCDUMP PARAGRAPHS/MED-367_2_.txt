For information about low back pain history, a close relative of each of the deceased was interviewed two to four weeks after the necropsy. RESULTS—Twenty one (22%) men and nine (20%) women had occluded arteries, and an additional 33 (35%) men and 17 (38%) women had narrowed arteries. The mean age for men with occluded or narrowed arteries, or both, was 50 years and for women 59 years.
