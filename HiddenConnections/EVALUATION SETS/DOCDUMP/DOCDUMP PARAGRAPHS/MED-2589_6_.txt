Concentrations of PCB congeners IUPAC no. 118, 138, 153, 156 and 180, HCB, Î²-HCH, trans-nonachlor and p, p'-DDE increased with increased age and were highest in women sampled early during the 4 year study period. This shows that older women and women sampled early in the study had experienced the highest life-time exposure levels, probably mainly during childhood and adolescence.
