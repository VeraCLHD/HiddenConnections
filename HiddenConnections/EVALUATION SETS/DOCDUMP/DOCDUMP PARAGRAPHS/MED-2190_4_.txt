PARTICIPANTS: Examination of the association 25(OH)D levels and mortality from cardiovascular disease, stroke, and acute myocardial infarct was performed among 161 428 women and 86 146 men. MAIN OUTCOME MEASURES: A multivariate Cox regression analysis was used to compute hazard ratios for cardiovascular, stroke, and acute myocardial infarct mortality. RESULTS: Of 247 574 subjects, a total of 16 645 subjects died in the ensuing 0-7 years.
