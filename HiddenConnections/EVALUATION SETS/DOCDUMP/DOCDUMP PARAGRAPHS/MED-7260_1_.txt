Some, but not all, studies have shown that soy protein intake decreases total and low-density lipoprotein cholesterol and triglycerides and increases high-density lipoprotein cholesterol. The objective of this meta-analysis was to examine the effect of soy protein supplementation on serum lipid levels in adults. English language articles were retrieved by searching MEDLINE (1966 to February 2005) and the bibliographies of the retrieved articles.
