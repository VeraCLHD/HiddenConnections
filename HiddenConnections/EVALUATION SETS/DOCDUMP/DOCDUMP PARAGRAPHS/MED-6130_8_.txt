MAIN OUTCOME MEASURES: To scientifically and objectively find out if PDE5 inhibitors are associated with vision-threatening ocular complications. RESULTS: Eight case reports of serious PDE5 inhibitor-associated ocular complications were identified since January 2006 until February 2011. Case reports included cases of anterior and posterior nonarteritic ischemic optic neuropathy, central retinal vein occlusion, cilio-retinal artery occlusion, acute angle closure glaucoma and optic atrophy after sildenafil use.
