Heavy smokers ( > or = 20 cigarettes/daily) were 27.0% in 1960 as compared with 35.7% in 1991 (:NS); 5.4% of the subjects in 1960 had light physical activity (PA) as compared with 14.3% in 1991 (P < 0.01); 74.7% of the subjects were farmers in 1960 as compared with 43.6% in 1991 (P < 0.1). The prevalence of CHD was 0.7% in 1960 as compared with 9.5% in 1991 (P < 0.001). Hypertensive heart disease was found in 3.4% of the subjects in 1960 and 4.8% in 1991 (NS).
