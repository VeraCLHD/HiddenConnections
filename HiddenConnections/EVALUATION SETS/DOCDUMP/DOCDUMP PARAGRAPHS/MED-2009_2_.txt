BPA exposure is plausibly linked to childhood obesity, but evidence is lacking to date. OBJECTIVE: To examine associations between urinary BPA concentration and body mass outcomes in children. DESIGN, SETTING, AND PARTICIPANTS: Cross-sectional analysis of a nationally representative subsample of 2838 participants aged 6 through 19 years randomly selected for measurement of urinary BPA concentration in the 2003-2008 National Health and Nutrition Examination Surveys.
