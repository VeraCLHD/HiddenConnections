Objective To determine the effect of cigarette smoking on α4β2* nAChR occupancy in tobacco-dependent smokers. Design Fourteen 2-FA PET scanning sessions were performed. During the PET scanning sessions, subjects smoked 1 of 5 amounts (none, 1 puff, 3 puffs, 1 full cigarette, or to satiety [2½ to 3 cigarettes]).
