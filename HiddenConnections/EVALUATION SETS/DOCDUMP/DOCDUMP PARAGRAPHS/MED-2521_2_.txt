The extracts from pigmented rice are used as natural food colorants in bread, ice cream, and liquor as well as functional food. The pigmented rice is mainly black, red, and dark purple rice, and contains a variety of flavones, tannin, phenolics, sterols, tocols, Î³-oryzanols, amino acids, and essential oils. Anthocyanins are thought as major functional components of pigmented rice.
