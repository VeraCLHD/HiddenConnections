METHODS: Subjects were derived from random, population-based samples previously studied in surveys carried out in 1972, 1977, 1982, and 1987. After an average follow-up of 21 years, 1,449 subjects aged 65 to 79 years were reexamined in 1998. RESULTS: Eighty-two subjects, 6.1% of the population (average age, 72 years) met the criteria for MCI.
