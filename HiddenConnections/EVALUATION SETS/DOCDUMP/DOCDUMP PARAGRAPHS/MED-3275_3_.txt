This investigation examined the association between cerebrovascular disease and the pathologic manifestations of AD in an autopsy series. METHODS: This was a cross-sectional study using data from the United States National Alzheimer's Coordinating Center database. The primary analysis included 1,054 individuals with clinical information and semiquantitative neuropathologic measurements: 921 had AD as the primary neuropathologic diagnosis and 133 were considered neuropathologically normal.
