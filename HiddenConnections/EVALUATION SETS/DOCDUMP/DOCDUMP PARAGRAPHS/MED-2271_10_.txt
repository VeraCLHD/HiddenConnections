SUBJECTS: US children (n 1961) aged 2-9 years with complete responses on the dietary frequency questionnaire. RESULTS: Intakes of EFF beverages were significantly associated with asthma in 2-9-year-olds. Adjusted odds of asthma in children consuming EFF beverages ≥5 times/week was more than five times that in children consuming these beverages ≤1 time/month (OR=5·29, P=0·012).
