DESIGN: A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, parallel study. SETTING: Kumamoto in Japan. SUBJECTS: Healthy adult men (30-60 years) with borderline hepatitis who had one or more of serum gamma-glutamyl transferase (GGT), aspertate aminotransferase (AST) and alanine aminotransferase (ALT) levels over normal ranges, and who were negative for hepatitis virus were openly recruited by an advertisement.
