DATA SOURCES: PubMed, TOXLINE, and Web of Science were searched using the term phthalate(s) combined with the keywords allergy, asthma, atopy, and inflammation. STUDY SELECTIONS: Articles were selected based on relevance to the goals of this review. Studies that involved humans were prioritized, including routes and levels of exposure, developmental and early-life exposures, immunotoxicity, and the development of allergic disease.
