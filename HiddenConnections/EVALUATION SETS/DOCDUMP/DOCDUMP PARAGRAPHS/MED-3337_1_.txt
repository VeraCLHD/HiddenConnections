Many see consuming fruit smoothies as a way of achieving this. OBJECTIVE: To ascertain the potential or otherwise for fruit smoothies to bring about dental erosion. DESIGN: Laboratory study.Method This was an in vitro investigation in which five varieties of shop bought fruit smoothies, including a 'thickie' were investigated, with respect to their initial pH, titratable acidity and effect upon exposure to the surface microhardness and profile of extracted human teeth.
