While metformin and berberine appear to have the greatest utility as clinical AMPK activators—as reflected by their efficacy in diabetes management—regular ingestion of vinegar, as well as moderate alcohol consumption, may also achieve a modest degree of health-protective AMPK activation. The activation of AMPK achievable with any of these measures may be potentiated by clinical doses of the drug salicylate, which can bind to AMPK and activate it allosterically.
