Statistical analyses were performed using the Mann-Whitney U test and repeated measures analysis of variance. Corresponding values for those with type 2 diabetes were very similar (46.1 ± 6.2, 43.8 ± 7.7, 51.8 ± 6.9, 50.2 ± 3.9 and 53.0 ± 6.0).
