The babies had scheduled physical examinations and their cognitive development, visual acuity, and vital signs to be checked. Adverse events were also recorded. RESULTS: The mean Mental Development Index (MDI) scores for the supplementation and non-supplementation groups were 96.1 +/- 8.6 and 91.7 +/- 10.4 respectively at 6 months and 98.7 +/- 8.0 and 90.5 +/- 6.9 respectively at 1 year.
