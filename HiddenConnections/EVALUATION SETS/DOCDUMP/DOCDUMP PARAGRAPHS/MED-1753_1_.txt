OBJECTIVE: To perform a meta-analysis of all published studies to assess the associations of circulating 25(OH)D levels measured at or near the time of diagnosis and outcomes for cancer patients. DATA SOURCES: Searches of the PubMed and MEDLINE databases were performed and updated to December 2013. STUDY SELECTION: Studies reporting an association between circulating 25(OH)D levels at or near the time of diagnosis and outcomes for the patients were included.
