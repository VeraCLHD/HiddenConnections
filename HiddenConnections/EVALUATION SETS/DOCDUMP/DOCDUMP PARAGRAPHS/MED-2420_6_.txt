Overall summary OR showed a 10% reduction in risk of breast cancer associated with high intake of citrus fruits (summary OR, 0.90; 95% CI, 0.85-0.96; p<0.001); results were consistent across the studies (I2=0). Visual inspection of the results did not suggest a publication bias. Conclusion Pooled results from observational studies showed an inverse association between citrus fruits intake and the risk of breast cancer.
