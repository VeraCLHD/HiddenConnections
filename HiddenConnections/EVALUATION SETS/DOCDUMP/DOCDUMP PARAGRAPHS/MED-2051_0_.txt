Abstract OBJECTIVE: This study aimed to evaluate the efficacy of Hop on early menopausal symptoms and hot flashes. METHODS: In this randomized controlled trial, 120 women were randomly allocated into two groups, receiving the Hop or placebo tablets for 12 weeks. Early menopausal symptoms were assessed using Greene scale and hot flashes were recorded in a diary before, and 4, 8 and 12 weeks after intervention.
