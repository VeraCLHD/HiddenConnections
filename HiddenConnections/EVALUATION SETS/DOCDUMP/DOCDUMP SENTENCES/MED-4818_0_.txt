abstract sulforaphane [1-isothiocyanate-(4r)-(methylsulfinyl)butane] is a natural dietary isothiocyanate produced by the enzymatic action of the myrosinase on glucopharanin, a 4-methylsulfinylbutyl glucosinolate contained in cruciferous vegetables of the genus brassica such as broccoli, brussel sprouts, and cabbage.
