abstract to study the effects of grapefruit and grapefruit products on body weight and metabolic syndrome, 91 obese patients were randomized to either placebo capsules and 7 ounces (207 ml) of apple juice, grapefruit capsules with 7 ounces (207 ml) of apple juice, 8 ounces (237 ml) of grapefruit juice with placebo capsule, or half of a fresh grapefruit with a placebo capsule three times a day before each meal.
