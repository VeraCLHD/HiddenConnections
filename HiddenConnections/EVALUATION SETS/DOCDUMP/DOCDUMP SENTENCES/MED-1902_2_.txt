we followed up participants from three prospective cohorts in the united kingdom: 22,421 participants from the european prospective investigation into cancer and nutrition (epic)-norfolk cohort (1993–2012), 52,625 participants from the epic-oxford cohort (1993–2012), and 7440 participants from the whitehall ii cohort (1991–2012), all reporting no history of heart attack, stroke, or cancer when entering these studies.
