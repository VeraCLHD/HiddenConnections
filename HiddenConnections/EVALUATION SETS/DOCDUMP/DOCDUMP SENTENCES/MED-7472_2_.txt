design: 20 healthy men followed three different diets for 4 weeks each, according to a randomized crossover design: western diet: 15% protein, 47% carbohydrates (cho), 38% fat (22% saturated fatty acid (sfa)); mediterranean diet: 15% protein, 47% cho, 38% fat (24% monounsaturated fatty acid (mufa)); cho-rich and n-3 diet: 15% protein, 55% cho, <30% fat (8% polyunsaturated fatty acid (pufa)).
