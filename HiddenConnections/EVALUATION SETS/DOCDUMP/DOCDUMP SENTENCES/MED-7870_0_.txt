abstract background/aims: the objective of the study was to collect data on dietary fat intake of omnivores, vegetarians, vegans and semi-omnivores as well as its impact on n-3 and n-6 fatty acids in long-term markers such as sphingolipids, phosphatidylcholine (pc), phosphatidylserine (ps), phosphatidylethanolamine (pe) as well as the calculated sphingo- and phospholipids (spl) of erythrocytes.
