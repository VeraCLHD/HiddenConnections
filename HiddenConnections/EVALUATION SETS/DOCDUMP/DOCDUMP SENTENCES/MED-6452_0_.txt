abstract purpose the objective of this study was to formulate and evaluate freeze-dried black raspberry (fbr) ethanol extract (re) loaded poly(dl-lactic-co-glycolic acid) (plga) and poly(dl-lactic acid) (pla) injectable millicylindrical implants for sustained delivery of chemopreventive fbr anthocyanins (cyanidin-3-sambubioside (cs), cyanidin-3-glucoside (cg) and cyanidin-3-rutinoside (cr)).
