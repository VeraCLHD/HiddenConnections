abstract aim: the efficacy of optimal doses of highly bioavailable (-)-hydroxycitric acid (hca-sx) alone and in combination with niacin-bound chromium (nbc) and a standardized gymnema sylvestre extract (gse) on weight loss in moderately obese subjects was evaluated by monitoring changes in body weight, body mass index (bmi), appetite, lipid profiles, serum leptin and excretion of urinary fat metabolites.
